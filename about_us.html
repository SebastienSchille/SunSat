We are Project SunSat.

We are a multidisciplinary team of over 25 undergraduate and postgraduate students with specialties ranging from avionic systems design to business management. SunSat aims to have a student designed and built experiment in orbit by 2025.

Team Nova

The Nova team is made up of x students and work to our to send a student made satellite to LEO by 2050. Prototyping and testing is carried out through the use of a high altitude balloon.

Team Management

The Management team is vital to SunSat's success. They ensure that projects are working within budgets and time restraints while enabling communication between teams. Communication between partners is upheld by the team.

Team CanSat

The CanSat team work on creating a simulation of a real satellite integrated within the volume and shape of a soft drinks can. These First year engineering will see their satellite launched from the Machrihanish airbase for the Mach-21 CanSat competion

WHY JOIN US?

Improve your Engineering Knowledge

Students will build on their course knowledge through the use of industry level software, The Diamond laboratories at The University of Sheffield, independent research, and colleagues, all while working on engaging projects.

Learn about space science

Many of our team members hope to have a career in the space industry after university. Our projects will enable students to have an insight into the industry and learn important skills that will boost their CVs

Learn to work in a Team

All our project have a structural managent team, giving student to opportunity to work in leadership roles and learn how to communicate and work in team and report to team leaders.
