Phoenix Space

Phoenix Space is a ground breaking educational project to educate, empower and inspire disadvantaged youth through space science and an orbital experiment. They have worked closely with SunSat to take experiments to 30km+ through the HAB project to provide their students with a wealth of data. This will enable to student to learn about data analysis by using real world data.
