Project Nova.

SunSat aims to have a satellite (Nova-1) in orbit by 2025. Nova-1 will monitor the rate of deforestation from Low Earth Orbit (LEO) by carrying a highresolution camera to facilitate Earth Observation.

Nova-1 will be a demonstration that a student-built satellite is feasible. Future Nova satellites will be designed with flexibility and modularity in mind which means that the same core systems can be used with a variety of experiments with little to no alteration, enabling fast mission turnarounds.
